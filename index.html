biography * graphic design & co.

SERENA DE MOLA is a graphic designer based in Italy, between Venice and Bari. Her practice investigates the right balance between theory and practice in order to create inclusive and intersectional scenarios. Her master degree project explores the relationship between graphic design, indipendent magazines and queer studies. She’s currently looking for a internship in the EU.
