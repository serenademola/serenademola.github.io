pagine queer. le riviste indipendenti come luogo d'intersezione. iuav master thesis, 2023. editorial & research. the research investigates the connection between semiosphere, stereotype and lbtqia+ political movements focusing on visual and social contexts, through six case studies of european indipendent magazines. it reflects on how design can also be transformative and resist the visual and social canon — conceiving independent magazines as a place where strategies of resistance can be enacted by the queer community. supervisor saul marcadent. co-supervisor fiorella bulegato.

case studies with interviews and archive research. (1) la bollettina del cli (roma), interview with sara de giovanni at centro documentazione cassero, bologna. (2) speed demon queerzine (milan), interview with giulia vallicelli at compulsive archive, milan. (3) butt magazine (amsterdam). (4) girls like us magazine (brussels). (5) frute (udine), online interview with cecilia cappelli. (6) self-pleasure publishing (milan), online interview with jacopo miliani.
